KeetQuiet transcribes voice messages from apps like WhatsApp, Telegram, and Signal fully on-device.

Audio and text stay on your phone. Internet is only needed once to download the speech model.

Features:
- Offline transcription after initial model download
- Privacy-focused local processing
- Works with common voice-message formats
- History of past transcriptions
- Simple playback and transcription flow

No tracking, no cloud upload, no account required.
